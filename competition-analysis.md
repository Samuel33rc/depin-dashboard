## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Differentiation

1. **DIMO First** - Target DIMO vehicle owners who need a dashboard
2. **Telegram-Native** - Leverage Telegram crypto community
3. **Open Source** - Developers can contribute/modify
4. **$0 Free Tier** - No signup required, instant access

### Target Users

1. **DIMO vehicle owners** - No other tool tracks DIMO
2. **Helium operators** - Want Telegram alerts, not SMS
3. **Multi-DePIN users** - Want unified view (future: add more networks)
4. **Privacy-conscious** - No account needed, wallet-only
